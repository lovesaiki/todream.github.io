Bus And Letters

Synopsis

Every day, Hana rides the same bus... and every day, she finds mysterious letters left behind for "The Passenger." Encouraging, secretive, sometimes heartbreaking, the letters speak to her in ways no one else can.

Curious, she begins replying—never knowing who's reading her words. But as their invisible bond grows, Hana realizes the writer isn't just anyone... and time is running out before they might never meet.

Some connections can only be found in letters... on a bus.
