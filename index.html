Betting Despite Odds

In a city where luck can make you a king—or cost you everything, Kael is a mathematical prodigy with nothing to lose and everything to prove. When he stumbles into the underground world of high-skates gambling, he discovers that winning isn't just about skill—it's about reading people, surviving deception, and risking more than money. Guided by the enigmatic Selene, Kael must navigate dangerous rivals, shadowy alliances, and the seductive thrill of game. As the stakes rises, the line between fortune and survival blurs, and Kael learns that some bets are worth more than gold... and some odds are meant to be defied. A story of courage, cunning, and the gamble of life itself, "Betting Despite Odds" asks: how far would you go when the odds are stacked against you?

Bus & Lettes

Every day, Hana rides the same bus... and every day she finds mysterious letters left behind for "The Passenger." Encouraging, secretive, sometimes heartbreaking, the letters speak to her in ways no one else can. Curious, she begins replying—never knowing who's reading her words. But as their invisible bond grows, Hana realizes the writer isn't just anyone... and time is running out before they might never meet. Some connection can only be found in letters... on a bus.

Hopelessly Romantic Love

Lia doesn't just believe in love—she lives for it. From memorizing love poems to imagining meet-cutes in the most ordinary moments, she's the kind of girl who sees romance everywhere. To her, every glance could mean something, every conversation could be the start of forever. But there's one problem: she always falls in love alone. Whether it's the boy who only sees her as a friend, the one who likes someone else, or the one who leaves without explanation—Liea keeps giving her heart to stories that were never meant to be hers. Still, she refuses to change. Because if loving deeply is a mistake, it's one she's willing to make over and over again. Everything shifts when she meets Eli, someone who reads her like an open book and challenges her beliefs about love. For the first time, Lia begins to question— does love gave to be painful to be real? Or has she been chasing the wrong kind of story all along? As her feelings grow, Lia must face the hardest truth she's ever known: sometimes, being a hopeless romantic means learning when to hold on... and when to finally let go.
