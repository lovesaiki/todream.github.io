Hopelessly Romantic Love

Synopsis

Lia doesn’t just believe in love—she lives for it. From memorizing love poems to imagining meet-cutes in the most ordinary moments, she’s the kind of girl who sees romance everywhere. To her, every glance could mean something, every conversation could be the start of forever.

But there’s one problem: she always falls in love alone.

Whether it’s the boy who only sees her as a friend, the one who likes someone else, or the one who leaves without explanation—Lia keeps giving her heart to stories that were never meant to be hers. Still, she refuses to change. Because if loving deeply is a mistake, it’s one she’s willing to make over and over again.

Everything shifts when she meets Eli, someone who reads her like an open book and challenges her beliefs about love. For the first time, Lia begins to question—does love have to be painful to be real? Or has she been chasing the wrong kind of story all along?

As her feelings grow, Lia must face the hardest truth she’s ever known: sometimes, being a hopeless romantic means learning when to hold on… and when to finally let go.
