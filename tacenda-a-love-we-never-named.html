Tacenda: A Love We Never Named

Synopsis

Solenne and Kendrick are best friends bound by a love they never dared to confess.

Every silence between them carries the weight of what could have been, every glance a promise left unspoken.

Tacenda is a story of love preserved in silence… but when silence becomes the only language—is it protection, or surrender?
