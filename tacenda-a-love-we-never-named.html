Tacenda: A Love We Never Named

Synopsis

Solenne and Kendrick are best friends bound by a love they never dared to confess. Every silence between them carries the weight of what could have been, every glance a promise left unspoken.

As their bond deepens, fear keeps them from crossing the fragile line between friendship and love. On the day Solenne walks down the aisle, Kendrick is there — her constant, her almost, her never.

Tacenda is a story of love preserved in silence, of what endures when words are left unsaid.

But when silence becomes the only language between two hearts—is it protection, or surrender?
