Tacenda: A Love We Never Named

Synopsis

In a city where luck can make you a king—or cost you everything, Kael is a mathematical prodigy with nothing to lose and everything to prove. When he stumbles into the underground world of high-stakes gambling, he discovers that winning isn’t just about skill—it’s about reading people, surviving deception, and risking more than money.

Guided by the enigmatic Selene, Kael must navigate dangerous rivals, shadowy alliances, and the seductive thrill of the game. As the stakes rise, the line between fortune and survival blurs, and Kael learns that some bets are worth more than gold… and some odds are meant to be defied.

A story of courage, cunning, and the gamble of life itself, “Betting Despite Odds” asks: how far would you go when the odds are stacked against you?
